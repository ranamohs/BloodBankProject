You Know Your Blood
Can Give Me a Life ?

Definition

website that facilitates blood donation requests after verifying their authenticity by delivering them with privacy and transparency to volunteers wishing to donate blood and who are geographically close to the donor through the pre-defined geographical location in the volunteer profile

Requirements

  • The donor must be in good health and not suffer from any contagious diseases.
  • The age of the donor should be between 18-65 years.
  • The donor must weigh at least 50 kg.
  • The hemoglobin level for men should be 14-17 g, and for women it should be 12-14 g.
  • The pulse should be between 50-100 per minute.
  • That the temperature does not exceed 37 degrees Celsius. The average blood pressure is less than 120/80 mm Hg.

Mechanism of action

1

The patient in need of blood donation submits an online donor request form.


2

The application is received through the Hayah website.



3


The operating team communicates with the patient or the hospital to verify the validity of the request.
4

The application is published after electronic matching of blood groups of donors in the same city as the patient.
